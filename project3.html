Static Structural Analysis of Elastomeric Engine Mount: A Comprehensive Study on Deformation Evolution Due to Ageing

This project involved performing an in-depth structural analysis of elastomeric engine mounts, focusing on how they deform over time due to ageing and operational stress. Using Finite Element Analysis (FEA) techniques, the project aimed to predict stress, strain, and deformation patterns under different load conditions.

The insights gained from the simulations were used to enhance the design's durability and extend the lifespan of the engine mounts. The findings contributed to better understanding the performance limits of elastomeric materials in automotive applications, ensuring that engine mounts can withstand prolonged operational cycles without compromising performance.
