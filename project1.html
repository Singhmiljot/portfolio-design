Lightweight Multifunction Hand Trolley

This project involved the design and development of a lightweight multifunction hand trolley. The goal was to create a product that was both functional and aesthetically pleasing, while also meeting the requirements of being lightweight and easy to maneuver.

The project involved the use of finite element analysis (FEA) to optimize the design and ensure that it could withstand various loads and stresses. The product was also designed with ergonomics in mind, to ensure that it was comfortable and easy to use.
