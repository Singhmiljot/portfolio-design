Optimising Horizontal Wind Turbine Design Concepts

This project focused on improving the efficiency and performance of horizontal wind turbines through design optimisation. Using advanced CAD modelling in SolidWorks and Finite Element Analysis (FEA) in ANSYS, the project aimed to enhance the aerodynamic performance of the turbine blades, thereby increasing power generation efficiency.

Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) techniques were applied to simulate airflow around the blades, enabling precise adjustments to improve energy capture. Collaboration with industry experts helped validate design modifications, ensuring the turbine adhered to ISO standards and provided sustainable, optimised energy production.
