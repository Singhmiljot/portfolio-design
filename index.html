About Me

As a Mechanical Design Engineer, I thrive on transforming complex ideas into tangible solutions that meet real-world demands. With a strong foundation in CAD, FEA, and hands-on product development, I specialise in creating 3D mechanical systems and components that tackle challenges across industries, from manufacturing to automotive technology. My passion lies in the fine balance between functionality and human-centred design, ensuring every solution not only performs but also enhances user experience. I believe in the power of collaboration, continuously learning, and pushing the boundaries of engineering. Whether it’s designing bespoke machinery for hazardous environments or working alongside teams to develop and resolving innovative systems, my approach is rooted in technical precision and creative problem-solving.Over the years, I have honed my skills through diverse projects, ranging from designing safety-critical mechanical systems to leading teams in the development of high-performance prototypes. My expertise spans several areas:

  • CAD & FEA: My proficiency in CAD tools and Finite Element Analysis allows me to create robust designs that are not only efficient but also safe under varying operational conditions. I’ve employed these skills in sectors requiring extreme precision, such as automobile technology and energy production.
  • Human-Centred Engineering: I hold a deep conviction that engineering should enhance the human experience. Every project I undertake is user-focused, ensuring that the end design seamlessly integrates into the user’s workflow or experience. This philosophy has been instrumental in developing systems that are as intuitive as they are innovative.
  • Cross-Industry Expertise: My career has spanned industries such as manufacturing, nuclear, and custom mechanical systems. These experiences have equipped me with the ability to navigate complex challenges, ensuring every solution is tailored to the specific needs of the industry and its end users.
  • Leadership & Collaboration: I enjoy working closely with interdisciplinary teams, driving projects forward from concept to implementation. My leadership has been vital in delivering on-time solutions, whether working as part of a team or independently driving project phases.
If you’re looking for an engineer who combines technical expertise with a passion for innovative, user-centred design, let’s connect and explore how we can bring your next project to life.
